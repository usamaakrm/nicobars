We’re a company that believes in culture as much as commerce, and that journeys are usually as worthy as destinations. These are the stories we’re telling through our product and through a community.

How Nicobar
Was Born

We believe India is filled with design talent that has not scaled and we wanted to create a culture that nurtures creativity. Plus, the journey of Royal Enfield and Good Earth is very inspiring: there’s so much to learn from the Lals, the founding family of those companies, on thinking long term, doing things out of passion, and building brands.

Naming our
brand

We already had a name and identity designed for our brand and launch was 4 months away. We brainstormed over 90 names associated with the Indian Ocean; Nicobar was not one of them! As is always the case, serendipity intervened (via my passion for golf). On a golf trip with seven close friends, a friend said ‘how about Nicobar’, and I just knew that was it.

WORK WITH US

core team works out of our lush garden studio in Delhi that we call the Treehouse, where designers sketch and drape, the digital teams write and code, and the gardeners plant broccoli and tomatoes, but our people are spread across the country, in our stores, and our warehouses.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Our aim has always been to create a line that champions easy, effortless style;
clothing that can be worn for years and homeware that’s with you for life.

Fabrics matter, as do fit and form. Whether in clothing or homeware: simplicity, versatility, and the ability to layer (and no big logos!) are the cornerstones of our collection. That’s where quality comes in.

We love details…little bits of whimsy to make our guests smile and to elevate the everyday. Look under the collars and the cuffs, at the thread in buttons… It's the little things that make the ordinary extraordinary.
